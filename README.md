## Features

- **311 Albums** from 162 artists spanning 1966-1989
- **Spotify Integration** - Click any album to play on Spotify
- **Search** - Find artists and albums instantly
- **Filter** - By genre (23 options) or decade
- **Sort** - By artist, album, or year
- **Responsive** - Works on desktop and mobile
- **No Backend** - Pure client-side, runs anywhere
